What is cybercrime?

Cybercrime(computer crime) can be defined as the use of a computer to further illegal activity( Dennis, n.d). An important aspect to cyber crime is that it can be classified as nonlocal which makes it harder for law enforncement becuase crimes can take place in totally jurisdiction and not all jurisdictions share the same laws ( Dennis, n.d).
Cybercrimes include but are not limited to:

  • Fraud
  • Trafficking of Child Pornography
  • Trafficking in Intellectual Property
  • Identity Theft
  • Distributed Denial of Service
  • Ransomeware

N.B This crimes existed before technology so techonology can be thought off as a means used to facilitate the crime.

This person also called hackers, are people who use computer systems for illegal activies such as the ones mentioned above.
